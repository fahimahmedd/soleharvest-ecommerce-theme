NIKE DUNK LOW

The Nike Dunk is undoubtedly one of the top 5 silhouettes we've ever seen from Nike. They first came out back in 1985, as the name suggests, as a basketball shoe in a high-top design. The Dunks are associated with the golden era of basketball as much as they are with the golden era of sneakers - in nearly 40 years on the market, they have had hundreds of collaborations, of which we must mention releases from the likes of Pigeon, Tiffany, FLOM and Heineken.

Explore the Nike Dunk Low on Footshop and discover the sneaker that has been shaping the perception of what it means to be cool for the last few decades.
